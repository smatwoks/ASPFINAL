    CAAQMS

    Continuous Ambient Air Quality Monitoring System

    The Continuous Ambient Air Quality Monitoring System (CAAQMS) provides real-time monitoring of air quality by measuring key pollutants like PM2.5, PM10, SO2, NO2, and CO. Our service includes both annual and half-yearly maintenance packages, ensuring your system operates optimally throughout the year. The annual service covers a 12-month period with two comprehensive system checks, while the half-yearly service spans 6 months, including one detailed inspection. Both options include regular calibration, data validation, and system checks to guarantee accurate data collection and compliance with regulatory standards. With CAAQMS, you gain access to crucial air quality data and benefit from continuous support, empowering your community to make informed decisions for a cleaner, healthier environment.

    CEMS

    Continuous Emission Monitoring System

    The Continuous Emission Monitoring System (CEMS) is a cutting-edge solution that continuously tracks and reports emissions from industrial processes, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. CEMS monitors key pollutants such as SO2, NOx, CO, CO2, and particulate matter, providing real-time data to help industries reduce their environmental impact. Our service includes installation, calibration, and regular maintenance to ensure accuracy and reliability. We offer both annual and half-yearly service plans, with the annual service covering a 12-month period with two comprehensive inspections, and the half-yearly service spanning 6 months with one detailed inspection. These services include performance checks and system calibration, ensuring your CEMS operates at peak efficiency. Choose CEMS for regulatory compliance and a commitment to a sustainable, healthier future.

    EQMS

    Continuous Effluent Quality Monitoring System

    The Continuous Effluent Quality Monitoring System (CEQMS) is designed to provide real-time monitoring of wastewater discharge, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. CEQMS continuously measures critical parameters such as pH, COD, BOD, TSS, and heavy metals, enabling industries to monitor and manage their effluent quality effectively. Our service includes installation, calibration, and ongoing maintenance to ensure the system's accuracy and reliability. We offer both annual and half-yearly service plans: the annual service covers a 12-month period with two comprehensive system inspections, while the half-yearly service spans 6 months with one detailed inspection. These services include regular system checks and calibrations, ensuring optimal performance. With CEQMS, you can proactively manage wastewater quality, safeguard the environment, and meet regulatory requirements.
