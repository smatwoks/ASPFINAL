    • GAS5000HPIDPR Plasma Ionization Detector

    This is a gas chromatograph equipped with a GAS5000HPIDÂ® plasma detector. The helium plasma ionization detector (HPID) is a highly sensitive detector that uses an extremely stable direct current discharge helium plasma as the ionization source. Helium has the highest excitation energy of all elements, and can ionize and detect all samples except neon with high sensitivity.

  • A detector with over 1,000 times the sensitivity of a TCD detector

    • Capable of measuring hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, methane, carbon monoxide, and carbon dioxide at sub-ppm levels

  • A detector with 10 times higher sensitivity than an FID detector

    • Hydrocarbons can be measured with higher sensitivity than FID without using detector gases such as combustion hydrogen or supporting air.
