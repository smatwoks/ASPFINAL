    COD-380R Automatic Online COD Analyzer

    This is an automated device that measures COD (chemical oxygen consumption), an important parameter for understanding the water quality of river water or industrial wastewater, based on the Japanese Industrial Standard JIS K0102 (industrial wastewater test method) and sewage test method. We have further evolved our COD automatic measurement device, which has a proven track record of high reliability and stability, and are now offering the "COD-3100" as a new model that meets user needs. While retaining the same ease of maintenance and measurement performance as previous models, we have reduced the amount of cleaning water used to about 1/20.

    TPN-580/TPN-580R Automatic Total Phosphorus/Total Nitrogen Analyzer

    This is an automated device that measures total phosphorus and total nitrogen, which are important indicators for understanding the water quality of river water or industrial wastewater, based on the Japanese Industrial Standard JIS K0102 (Industrial wastewater testing method). This device has inherited the ease of maintenance and measurement performance of the previous model, but has been upgraded to a device that will satisfy customers even more by incorporating a large LCD touch panel and TN/TP individual measurement functions. In addition, the TPN-580R, which requires only a small amount of reagent, requires half the amount of reagent for sample decomposition, making it possible to significantly reduce running costs for measurement.

    TN-310 Automatic Total Nitrogen Analyzer

    The automatic total nitrogen measuring device is a fully automated total nitrogen measuring method using the "contact pyrolysis/chemiluminescence method" adopted in JIS K0102. It can measure at intervals of several minutes and can be used for a wide range of purposes, from controlling and measuring nitrogen treatment to measuring discharged water.

    PHS-580i Automatic Phosphate Ion Analyzer

    This device fully automates the "molybdenum blue absorptiometry" adopted in JIS K 0102, and can measure phosphate ions in a short time. Understanding phosphate ion concentration is important for wastewater treatment process management and various boiler water management, and the PHS-580i uses its unique technology to achieve highly accurate long-term continuous measurement in order to meet such needs.

    HZ-680 Automatic Hydrazine Analyzer

    This device is a fully automated hydrazine measurement device based on JIS B8224, capable of measuring at intervals of approximately 15 minutes, and is ideal for constantly monitoring the concentration of hydrazine injected as a dissolved oxygen reducing agent in boiler water supply and controlling the injection amount.

    SIO-780 Automatic Silica Analyzer

    This device is a fully automated silica measurement system based on JIS B8224, capable of measurement at approximately 10-minute intervals, and is ideal for reducing the labor required for boiler maintenance and management, as it can continuously measure silica leaks from ion exchange equipment used to produce boiler make-up water and silica concentration in boiler water.

    TCN-580 Automatic Total cyanogen Analyzer

    Cyanide is measured by the distillation operation - pyridine pyrazolone absorptiometry method specified in JIS K0102, but this measurement method requires skill and takes several hours, so a simple and reliable measurement method was desired. TCN-580 can measure total cyanide in a sample within 30 minutes, and can measure concentrations from 0.01 mg/L, so it can be widely used not only for automatic monitoring of total cyanide, but also for labor-saving measurements to replace manual analysis.

    CR-680 Automatic Hexavalent chromium Analyzer
